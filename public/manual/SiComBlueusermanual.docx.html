user manual

Zone1 : product information zone

Zone2 : connection status zone

Zone3 : Add or Remove Node zone

Zone4 : reset choices zone

Zone5 : Discover/Disconnect device zone

Zone6 : your customized Characteristics/Services zone

Once landed on SiComBlue.herokuapp.com, you will see the above web page.

Zone 1 : product information zone. you can find the version of the product, release note (with link) and profile example (with link). I am putting a trial welcoming notes seems the timing is a bit shifted, still investigating, to be updated >>..

Zone 2 : connection status table (with profile selection and upload interface). The information showing here is the connection status regarding the nodes connected. But before connect, you will need to choose the profile (default I provided 4 selections once you click on the select profile bar: pop down list included BBC Microbit, ST BlueST, ST BlueST_full, ST BlueNRG). Try to select any of them, you will see the page refreshed upon your selection (if not, means your selected or uploaded profile is not recognized by this tool, please re-do the slection/edit you profile then re-uploading):

Then you may want to go to Zone 5 (as the push button will be enabled once profile recognized). As below showing a step to make connection after pushed discoverDevice button in Zone 5:

After selected the right device from the pop up window, your web will be connected to your device. Check Zone 2 status and it should be updated to true for (coonected, and Services Discovery Completed colomn) and device name will be showed accordingly. if not, redo the discoverDevice process.

Now you could go to Zone 6 customizing your Characteristic choices. Note that any checkbox click will resulting a Service and Characteristics re-shuffle and matching, as you may see when profile loaded the services and chars are not yet bond together, while after service discovery completion, your click will impose the discovered Services and Char shuffle and matching. It should be understandable, as some char/services may disappear if your connected device does not expose those char/services.

Step forward, keep trying N/R/W (N-notification, R-read, W-writing) click, and you can push the Act! Button accordingly to trigger immediate NRW action per your choice upon related Characteristic. Or you could push the ProcessAllCharSelections button to perform a simutanous all the exposed Characteristic NRW actions. See below pic:

After those test, if you want to save your received notification data, just simplely click disconnect button in Zone 5 and say YES to the download request, data will be saved on your download file.
